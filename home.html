The leading online marketplace for fashion items

There are over 7 billion people in our planet and 99.9% percent of them put on clothing and fashionable items; thus, Clothing is too much a necessity to be limited - in selection, time, profit, convenience and generally productivity. With that thought in our mind, Dressbook was born. Our mission is driving limitless commerce by putting people at the center of commerce and building communities bound by love. Our vision is Connecting one buyer to every seller and one seller to every buyer in the clothing and fashion industry.

Every Buyer, Every Seller, One Platform

As the world transitions into a global community, the internet has made it so much easier to find what we want because we can shop globally instead of being limited to whatever is in our local area. But different surveys have shown that many people especially those of African countries, are left behind due to complexity, cost and other factors. Dressbook is giving everyone from the buyer to the seller the gift of that global community by breaking the complexity of the internet and displacing into the hands of the people. Simply, Dressbook is fashion ecommerce of the people, by the people and for the people
